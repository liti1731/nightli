About us

nightli™️ is a mobile app and web application built by Tribed Community AB in Stockholm, Sweden.


Time has finally come to digitize all stages of “going out” through gamification. With the help of big data and a playful user experience nightli will be the first app to bring nightlife as we know it today, to a community platform that raises guest loyalty for nightclub owners. Beneficiaries of our solution will not only be the clubs, but also the visitors thanks to our point-based system. Therefore, with the help of smart algorithms our goal is to grow world's nightlife in a fair, safeand humorous way through technology. We believe nightlife is not a closedguestlist. But a community, where everyone is presented with an equal opportunity!

News

Project is supported by KTH Innovation.


Among many strong applicants nightliTM was one of the few projects selected to participate in the KTH innovation pre-incubator program starting Fall 2019. The group of start-ups selected (Batch 10) are presented with office space, juridical support, experienced business advisors and more. For Tribeds business, this support provides well recieved validation of project nightliTM and our mission of building a digital nightlife of the future. In addition, working alongside other successful start-ups inspires us to continue innovating and build a forward-thinking company culture.
